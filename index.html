WHAT WE USE

We only use vegetable tanned leather from Italy.
Vegetable tanned leather represents the highest quality, and durability in leather goods. It takes about 40 days to process from raw hides, with natural tannins extracted from plants. The final leather is natural. As such, it is too normal to experiance changes with time.
The leather is then brought to our studio, crafted by using a unique and special process all created by hand that brings you style, quality, and personality unlike anything that you have seen.

LEATHER CARE

Please prevent leather from liquid as it is not waterproof.
Water spots may retain even when it's dried. Fortunately, black or darker leather is not likely to subject to this.
If you would like to clean minor dirts on leather surface, please use a sponge and a very small amount of mild soap. Do not use a hairdrier. Let the leather dry naturally and apply conditioner. Some leather maybe darkened by conditioners. You may want to test it on inconspicuous spot.
